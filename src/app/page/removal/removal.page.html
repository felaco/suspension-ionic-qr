Rear Suspension Removal

LME-SWP-00025 - pagina 1369

  • Follow the haul truck safety precautions [Reference: Safety, page 45]
CAUTION The rear suspension handling tool is not validated for the removal of the rear suspension. During the rear suspension removal procedure, use a lifting device that can safely lift and support the rear suspension weight. NOTICE The minimum weight of each filled rear suspension is 895kg. Use an approved lift device that can safely lift and support the component weight {{ tool.name }}

Step 1

Lift and support the rear end of the chassis until the rear suspensions are fully extended

NOTICE The weight of the rear axle must remain on the ground

Step 2

Fully discharge the nitrogen from both rear suspensions

Step 3

Disconnect the grease lines (6) and electrical connections from the supsension

Step 4

Remove the rod protection cover, if necessary

CAUTION
  • Damage to the piston rod can occur when the protective cover is removed
  • Be careful not to damage the piston rod surface when workin on the suspension with the protective cover removed

Step 5

Safely and securely attach the suspension to the lifting device

Step 6

Remove the cap screws (5), whashers (4) and retainer (3)

NOTICE

The minimum weight of each pin is 66 kg. Use an approved lift device that can safely lift and support the component weight

Step 7

Remove the two suspension pins (2) using the pin puller

Step 8

With the suspension safely secured to the lifting device, carefully lower the suspension from the haul truck

Step 9

Remove the rear suspension from the lifting device and move the suspension to a suitable shipping pallet.

Warning

If the suspension is leaking oil, drain the oil by removing the plug from the oil fill port

  • Fully collapse the supension and secure it for transport (i.e., using a band-strap through both eyes
  • Make sure the oil port and the gas valve are closed before shipping
