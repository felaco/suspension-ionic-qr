Rear Suspension Removal

LME-SWP-00025 - pagina 1369

{{ tool.name }}

Step 1

Assemble and setup the rear suspension handling tool, and secure with strap to the forklit.

See here how

Step 2

Safely and carefully move the forklift with the rear suspension installed on the rear suspension handling tool into position and align the upper eye bearing with the frame clevis.

NOTICE The minimum weigth of each pin is 66 kg (145 lb). -> Use an approved lift device that can safely lift and support the component weight.

Step 3

Apply a thin coat of anti-seize ti the suspension pin (2) and install on the upper eye.

Step 4

Lubricate the cap screws (5) with anti-seize.

Step 5

Install the retainer (3), washers (4), and cap screws (5).

Step 6

Tighten the cap screws (5) to 925 N*m (682 lbf ft).

Step 7

Connect the grease lines (6) and electrical connections to the suspension (1).
